About our Website:

The purpose of this website is to give legitimate information about COVID-19. This will
also serve as an eye opener on what happens in the present. website will be a way for
anyone to learn anything that is related to COVID-19. Any user that wants to learn more
about COVID-19 can access our website and receive the necessary information for both
their safety and for fighting misinformation.

- Keep a safe distance (at least 1 meter) from others, even if they do not appear to be unwell.
- Wear a mask in public, especially if you can't separate yourself physically.
- Open or well-ventilated areas are preferable than closed ones. If you are indoors,
open a window.
- Hands should be washed often. Use soap and water or an alcohol-based hand rub
to clean your hands.
- When it's your turn, get vaccinated. In terms of immunization, follow local
recommendations.
- When you cough or sneeze, cover your nose and mouth with your elbow or a
tissue.
- If you feel one of COVID - 19 symptoms, it is better to stay at home.
